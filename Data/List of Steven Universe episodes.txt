List of Steven Universe episodes
Steven Universe is an American animated television series created by Rebecca Sugar for Cartoon Network. The series revolves around the eponymous Steven Universe (Zach Callison), who helps protect Beach City alongside the Crystal Gems: Garnet (Estelle), Amethyst (Michaela Dietz) and Pearl (Deedee Magno). The series was renewed in July 2015 for a third season.
As of July 17, 2015, 67 episodes of Steven Universe have aired. New episodes will resume in September 2015.
